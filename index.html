Risk Assessment & Mitigation

🔴 High Priority: R-01 Scope Creep (AI Tool)

Risk that the scope of the AI tool expands beyond initial requirements, causing development delays.

Mitigation: Strictly adhere to the initial requirements document. All change requests must go through a formal approval process with James.

🟡 Medium Priority: R-02 Conflicting Feedback (Creative Assets)

Conflicting feedback from stakeholders on creative assets leads to endless revision cycles and delays.

Mitigation: Implement a structured review process with a single point of contact (Helena) for consolidating feedback. James to be the final arbiter on conflicting feedback.

🟡 Medium Priority: R-03 Content Bottlenecks

The creation of content (copywriting) for the marketing assets takes longer than anticipated, creating a bottleneck for the design phase.

Mitigation: Dedicate a resource to content creation early in the project. Write the copy for the most critical assets first.

🟢 Low Priority: R-04 External Printing Delays

Delays from the external printing vendor jeopardize the availability of physical assets for the trip.

Mitigation: Select the printing vendor and get production timelines in Week 2. Submit final files with at least 3 buffer days before the vendor's deadline.

🟢 Low Priority: R-05 Team Member Unavailability

A key team member (Helena or Isabelle) becomes unavailable due to illness or other unforeseen circumstances.

Mitigation: Ensure all project documentation and work-in-progress is stored in a central, accessible location. Cross-train a backup resource on the basics of the AI tool.

🟢 Low Priority: R-06 AI Tool Bugs/Adoption

The final AI tool has critical bugs or is not adopted by the team due to a poor user experience.

Mitigation: Conduct thorough User Acceptance Testing (UAT) in Week 5. Create a simple user guide and hold a training session.
