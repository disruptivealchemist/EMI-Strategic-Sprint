Critical Success Factors
Early Engagement: Involvement in specification development phase
Multi-Stakeholder Approach: Address technical, commercial, and operational concerns
Reference Credibility: Strong case studies and peer recommendations
Technical Competence: Detailed specification and support capabilities
Partnership Approach: Long-term relationship focus beyond single transactions
Workflow Optimization Recommendations
For Distributors
Accelerated Evaluation: Provide comprehensive partnership packages upfront
Risk Mitigation: Offer pilot programs and graduated partnership terms
Technical Support: Ensure robust training and support systems
For Builders/Architects
Early Specification Support: Engage during design phase with technical consultation
Integration Focus: Emphasize workflow optimization and custom solutions
Project Partnership: Provide dedicated project support and coordination
For Procurement Authorities
Compliance Leadership: Proactive regulatory and standards compliance
Total Cost Focus: Emphasize lifecycle value and operational efficiency
Stakeholder Alignment: Address both technical and commercial requirements simultaneously
